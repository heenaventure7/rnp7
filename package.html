Pefect Tour Packages

Rajasthan
Half Day Jeep Safari
A half-day jeep safari can be further categorized as a morning safari and an evening safari. The morning safaris start around 0600 hours depending on the sunrise time and last for about 4.5-5 hours. After the morning safari, the guest heads back to the lodge/resort. You can freshen up, eat, relax for a bit and head back into the jungle for your evening safari. The evening safari lasts for about 5-6 hours depending on the sunset.
Rajasthan
Full Day Jeep Safari
A full-day jeep safari wherein your vehicle enters the park at the time of the opening of the park (depends on season) and stays till sunset. You spend the day within the national park, including lunch amidst the forest wilderness. You head back out 15 minutes after all the jeeps have exited the park post the evening safari. A full-day safari is also mostly taken by wildlife lovers or professionals who are following a particular species or documentarians who wish to film a specific family.
Rajasthan
Canter Safari
It is a 20-seater open bus, mostly taken by big groups or families. At a time, Canter safari can accommodate 20 people. It gives personal delight in sightseeing and the unique experience of exploring the magnificent vicinity of the park. The safari can be taken at two different times either early in the morning or late afternoon. It does not have the option of a full-day safari.
Rajasthan
Morning/Afternoon Regular Safari
It has the option of both Jeep and Canter. This happens twice a day for 3.5 hours in two shifts. Further categorized in morning and afternoon shifts.
Note: For solo travelers, we have the option of sharing a safari. We also provide safari according to individuals.
